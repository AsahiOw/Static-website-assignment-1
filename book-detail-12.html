The Final Girl Support Group

Narrator Lynnette Tarkington is part of the Final Girls support group, which has six members: Heather DeLuca, Marilyn Torres, Dani Shipman, Julia Campbell, and Lynnette. There used to be seven, but Chrissy Mercer is no longer with them because she was a traitor. As the novel opens, Lynnette sees images from a mass murder at Camp Red Lake, the retreat for survivors of violence that Adrienne Butler runs. There is only one survivor: Stephanie Fugate.
